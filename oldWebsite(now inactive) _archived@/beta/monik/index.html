Welcome to Aura

Aura consultants is a pioneer in the evolution of Requirement Established Placement (REP) into educational institutions worldwide. Every student envisions studying abroad for exposure into the multicultural world of education and achievement of personal and professional goals. We at Aura work prudently with our students from all walks of life to be placed in reputed institutions according to their choice and educational needs. Requirement based placement is the ideal way of selecting and enhancing your goal to study abroad. We help our students go through a step by step selection criterion before you decide to plunge into the ocean of available resources into studying abroad.
